What Does All-Access Include?

In addition to our excellent main gym area covered by the standard rate, which contains a full array of standard gym equipment including our dumbbells, cardio deck, and most of our machines, all-access membership includes:

  1. Our Powerlifting Room, with two monolifts, three competition benches, deadlift platform, specialized bars, boxes, bands, chains, boards, and everything else a powerlifter could want.
  2. Our Athletic Room, with an open turf run, glute-ham raise, reverse-hyperextension, ropes, bags, sleds, and other advanced training equipment.
  3. Our Strongman Room, featuring multiple lifting and loading platforms and a huge array of logs, stones, kegs, axles, and more. We're the best strongman training facility in the Midwest and one of the best-equipped in the nation.
  4. A Free weekly group training class to familiarize yourself with our equipment, learn new techniques to add to your routine, or just get the extra motivation a group provides.
  5. The ability to jump in with our powerlifting or strongman teams during any of their three weekly group sessions. Beginners welcome!
